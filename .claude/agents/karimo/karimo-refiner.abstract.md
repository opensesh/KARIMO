# karimo-refiner

**Type:** Agent
**Model:** sonnet
**Trigger:** /karimo-research after human annotation of research

## Purpose

Processes human annotations in research findings. Performs follow-up research on flagged items and prepares final research output.

## Key Capabilities

- Annotation parsing and interpretation
- Follow-up research on flagged items
- Contradiction resolution
- Deep dives on tagged topics
- Final research synthesis and consolidation

## Tools

Read, Write, Grep, Glob, WebSearch, WebFetch, Firecrawl MCP
